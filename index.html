Private access to part-time technical opportunities

Highly skilled employees are often restricted from seeking additional work due to time commitment issues or concerns with their full time role's contractual obligations.

Companies also struggle to access top-tier talent for work that a full time role is not needed for. They face the challenge of finding candidates willing to accept part time work and side gig platforms saturated with fake, low quality, or AI profiles.

The Shadow Roles Promise

We offer an exclusive hiring platform that ensures top tier candidates can take on additional work with flexible part-time engagements.

The platform will vet potential contracts and match employees with part time roles that work for them.

The platform also thoroughly vets all candidates, requiring an invite to join the platform and an additional screening process to ensure they are top talent.
